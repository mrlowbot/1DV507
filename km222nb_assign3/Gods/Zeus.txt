King of the gods, ruler of Mount Olympus, and god of the sky, weather, thunder, lightning, law, order, 
and justice. He is the youngest son of Cronus and Rhea. 
He overthrew Cronus and gained the sovereignty of heaven for himself. 
In art he is depicted as a regal, mature man with a sturdy figure and dark beard. 
His usual attributes are the royal scepter and the lightning bolt. 
His sacred animals include the eagle and the bull. His Roman counterpart is Jupiter, also known as Jove.
